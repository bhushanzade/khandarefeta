About Turban (Feta)

Turban is a pioneer in crafting traditional turbans in India. We proudly represent India's cultural heritage through our expertly crafted turbans, embodying elegance, tradition, and style.

Meet Shrikant Khandare

Shrikant Khandare started his turban-wearing and selling business in 2006. Today, he is recognized as the number one turban provider in Maharashtra, India, and has achieved remarkable feats globally.

Shrikant set a new world record in Russia, where 360 Indian representatives wore turbans in a moving plane. The pilot symbolically removed his Russian cap and placed it on Shrikant's head, marking a world first. During the event, a record-breaking tricolor was hoisted on the aircraft mid-air and on Russian soil.

Shrikant holds world records for both the smallest and largest turbans, with the latter being displayed on his own building. His turbans have graced many celebrities, including Sachin Tendulkar, and political leaders such as Shri Narendra Modi.
